The world of Sound Voltex comes to the hit indie rhythm game, Friday Night Funkin' (FNF)! For this project, I had to draw and animate a bunch of characters, backgrounds, and UI to all match the visual style of an early internet flash game. After studying the methods of FNF's lead artist, I pulled out Adobe Animate and my trusty drawing tablet, and ended up with my first foray into 'snap-to' animation, in the form of a mod for a game!

Over the course of this project, I've worked with several talented visionaries and coders to bring this mod to life. A great video demonstration of the game can be found here, for the first release. The game can also be downloaded and played for free here! There are countless videos on it, and our mod has become very popular!
