During my sophomore year at SUNY Poly, I applied for an open designer position at The Factory Times, the official magazine of the college. I figured that it would be an excellent opportunity to gain experience in the art of information design.

Near the end of my sophomore semester, I was asked if I wanted to take over the position of Lead Designer for next year. Of course I had to say yes! This is where I first started using Adobe InDesign, to assemble all the designs everyone made, produce the front and back covers, and create brand new table of contents & staff pages.

The semester after writing this, I'm slated to be the Publication Director of The Factory Times. That means I will be running all operations and representing the team as the leader of the organization.
